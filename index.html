About Siwel Design Studio

We are an Architectural Design Company based in Nairobi, Kenya.

We offer Architectural design services ranging from entire design projects, interior design projects to visualization projects as well as construction supervision and project management.

We deal with a variety of projects; Commercial, Residential, Offices, Stalls as well as pure 3D visualization projects providing our clients with accurate 3D models, designs and renders that show the actual completed look of the project.

Our Services

Architecture

From initial feasibility studies to final construction administration, we guide you through every stage of the architectural process with expertise and dedication. Our services include conceptual design, schematic design, and design development, ensuring that your vision is translated into detailed plans and specifications.

01
Interior Design

With a keen eye for aesthetics and a deep understanding of spatial dynamics, we collaborate closely with you to craft interiors that reflect your unique personality and lifestyle. From selecting the perfect color palette to choosing furnishings and finishes that complement your vision, we guide you through every step of the design process.

02
Urban Design

Our urban design services, led by our experienced team of architects and planners, aim to create vibrant, inclusive spaces that enrich the lives of residents and visitors alike. From revitalizing neighborhoods to master planning entire urban districts, we approach each project with a holistic perspective, considering factors such as transportation, public spaces, and environmental impact.
